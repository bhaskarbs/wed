About Us

World Class

Two Innovative Research Institutes

The Discovery Building houses twin interdisciplinary research institutes.

The Morgridge Institute for Research is a private, nonprofit research institute dedicated to improving human health.

LEARN MORE

The public Wisconsin Institute for Discovery is a transdisciplinary research institute and part of UW–Madison.
